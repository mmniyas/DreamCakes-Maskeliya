Our Story

Our story began in September 2012, born from a heartfelt passion to create the most delicious and beautiful cakes that add joy to your special moments. Every cake is crafted by Fathima Shihana Niyas with love, using the finest ingredients and a sprinkle of homemade magic.

From humble beginnings in our own kitchen, we’ve grown into a beloved bakery in Maskeliya—celebrated for our unique designs, unforgettable flavors, and trusted quality. Today, we proudly serve not only local customers but also families abroad who place orders for their loved ones here in Sri Lanka. Because we believe every celebration deserves a dream cake—and we’re here to make yours perfect.
